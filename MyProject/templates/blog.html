Building a Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis Web App

In the age of technology and information, access to accurate and timely healthcare is more critical than ever...

The Problem

People often experience symptoms and want quick answers about their health concerns. Differentiating between diseases with overlapping symptoms can be challenging.

The Solution

  1. Symptom Input: Users enter symptoms via an intuitive interface.
  2. Machine Learning Model: A Decision Tree Classifier predicts likely diseases.
  3. Prediction: Instant feedback on potential health concerns.
  4. Additional Info: Descriptions, precautions, and lifestyle tips accompany each prediction.

Key Features

  • User-Friendly Interface
  • Accurate Predictions
  • Comprehensive Information
  • Educational Content

Conclusion

Our Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis Web App brings the power of machine learning and healthcare information to your fingertips. It’s a step forward in making healthcare more accessible and user-centric worldwide.
